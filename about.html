About us

 

 

Foundations

The base of our server started when our original gaming community, the Lost Legion, demoted us from staff positions. We then left, and with the resources of the Important Three, put together this server from the ground up.

 

The Important Three

After the demotion of all of our friends and staff at Lost Legion, Garnite, NascarNick, and Intro_Flaw set out to make a server that would stand out to the rest of the world.

 

The Setup

After a few hours of research, and Nick's history in server ownership, we made a decision to go with Joe's Datacenter. After around 24 hours, we had a Dedicated Server at our disposal and from there we began to build up an actual gaming community.

 

The Originals

After Nick got a TeamSpeak3 Server up and running on our server, we had ex-staff and friends that we met on Lost Legion join our server. These people were FatherHells, Denali, Crona, Bullseye5420, Texas, Midnight the Neko-niisan, NerdyPanda(Aaron), Heroofthemall, Drum Beat, Bubski, Sadistyk, Issei, Lone Wolf, Ninja Sniper, Pilsner, Scorpean, and Professor Stalin. After everyone got into the server, we then pushed forth into making what you now know as CounterParts Gaming
